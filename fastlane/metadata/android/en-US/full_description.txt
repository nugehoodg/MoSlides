MoSlides is a lightweight and powerful presentation app that lets you create professional slides using Markdown.

Designed for simplicity and speed, MoSlides removes the complexity of traditional presentation tools and focuses on what matters most: your content.

✨ Features:
- Write slides using clean Markdown syntax
- Real-time preview of your presentation
- Powered by MARP (Markdown Presentation Ecosystem)
- Export presentations to PDF
- Minimal and distraction-free interface
- Customizeable slides theme
- Customizeable slides size
- Customizeable slides font size

🎯 Perfect for:
- Students and educators
- Developers and technical speakers
- Anyone who prefers writing over dragging UI elements

MoSlides is fully open-source and respects your privacy. No ads, no tracking, just a clean experience.
